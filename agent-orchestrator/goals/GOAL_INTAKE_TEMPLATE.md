# Goal Intake Template

Use this template when giving the Lead Agent a new owner goal.

## North-Star Goal

What outcome should POLY reach?

## Product Scope

What user/product experience should change?

## Backend Scope

What APIs, services, schemas, or data behavior are in scope?

## Bot Scope

What bot, reference sync, market-making, supervisor, or dry-run behavior is in scope?

## UI Scope

What pages, components, flows, or responsive states are in scope?

## Validation Requirements

What must be tested or evidenced?

## Acceptance Criteria

What exactly counts as done?

## Scorecard Target

What score, readiness classification, or capability state should be reached?

## Blocked Areas

What must not be touched?

## Allowed Areas

What can be changed without asking again?

## Priority Order

List the priority order if multiple phases are needed.

## What Counts As Done

State the final stopping condition.

## Notes

Add extra constraints, deployment rules, or safety rules.
